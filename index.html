What Illuminate does

A simple business with one goal: make lighting that looks clean, feels warm, and works in real spaces.

Mission

To bring thoughtfully designed, minimal, eco-friendly lighting into the dark, neglected corners of customers' workspaces and homes.

Business

Illuminate makes 3D-printed lamps that are bright, affordable, and stylish, with options that blend into a room or stand out as a focal point.

Value

Customers get a local, student-made product, immediate booth demos, and a polished minimalist look without a luxury price tag.
